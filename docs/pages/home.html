The empire is not built by legions, but by cart.

Samnite Supply Lines is a compact strategy puzzle about the quiet machinery of power. Set during the Third Samnite War in the Roman Republic, you are not a general commanding legions—you are the unseen hand that keeps Rome fed.

Your task is simple to describe and tricky to master:

  1. Find Rome hidden somewhere beyond the fog.
  2. Build farms to harvest wheat.
  3. Deliver grain before it spoils.

Each turn, wheat moves automatically toward Rome along the routes you’ve uncovered.
Each harvest has a limited lifespan.
Each decision shapes the fate of your supply network.

Random disruptions—raiding parties, tribute demands, shifting opportunities—force you to adapt and rethink your routes. The challenge is not war, but logistics under pressure.

This game is the first step in the Latent Hegemony™ series, a long‑term exploration of systems, influence, and the structures that shape civilizations. Future entries will expand into city‑building, provincial management, and eventually full grand strategy.

For now, the goal is simple:
